Features of the Curriculum

Eclectic Approach

This refers to the use of multiple approaches through different subjects to help a child grow in all areas of development.

Thematic Curriculum

It is an approach which involves integrating different areas of a curriculum around a topic thereby making learning more relevant for learners.

Developmental Milestones

It can be simply put as checkpoints in a child's development to understand the growth at a particular age. The main areas of developmental milestones include the role of physical, mental, social, language, and cognition at each stage in the child's growth.

Cross-Curriculum Approach

In traditional systems, all subjects are dealt with in isolation. While teaching one subject, using inputs from other subjects helps a child gain much deeper knowledge in learning concepts and skills.

Competency based approach

Most curriculums in India follow either knowledge based curriculum or skill based curriculum. Competency based curriculum refers to an amalgamation of knowledge, skill and attitude.

Social & Emotional Development

This refers to how a child deals with his/her own emotions and emotions of others when alone and around society.

Teacher Training Program

Training of teachers is an integral part of the system and is not taken as a generic approach for all the schools we associate with. We dont believe in one shoe fits all, hence, we carefully monitor, observe and discuss a schools need before suggesting any trainings to our partner schools.

Our training team comprises of trainers from educational field collectively with many years of experience in areas like classroom teaching, teaching strategies, developing curriculum, training teachers for reaching out to students, building teacher evaluation programs, to name a few.

At EdNew Solutions Pvt. Ltd., we have a bank of over 50 training programmes with an objective of regular growth of teachers and imbibing techniques that make teaching-learning fun and effortless, through hundreds of classroom teaching strategies, classroom management strategies along with usage of innovative tools and techniques. Our trainings are research-based and cover the various methodologies and the latest pedagogical practices prevalent across the globe.

The features of all the trainings and workshops are as follows:

  • Hands-on, comprehensive and focused on real-life situations
  • Activity-based, usage of strategies, and tools and techniques that makes learning effortless and lasting
  • Provides a complete know-how of each and every aspect of the teaching-learning process, their implementation, additional skills and insights
  • Simulates classroom environment
